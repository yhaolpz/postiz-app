# Tiny Agent bilingual longform

## Message

AI Agent reliability improves when uncertainty, clarification, evidence verification, and abstention are treated as valid outcomes instead of rewarding an answer at all costs.

## Audience

Knowledge workers, creators, product and operations teams, analysts, founders, and developers who delegate factual work to AI Agents.

## Visual direction

Paper-gray whiteboard world using the active Tiny Agent v2 identity, voice-synced kinetic question opening, seven numbered chapters, diverse character and prop compositions, generated scene art, and a dedicated fixed-language outro.

## Constraints

The two languages share content truth and visual intent but use independent scripts, TTS, VTT, scene timing, animation timing, and QA. English is published through local Postiz; Chinese remains local.
