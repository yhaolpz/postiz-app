## Confirmed source facts

- Everyday work already has context in calendars, messages, emails, documents, dashboards, spreadsheets, trackers, decks, and discussion history.
- The recommended starting point is to provide the materials the team already uses and request the first usable version of a concrete artifact.
- Example artifacts include briefs, summaries, decks, workbooks, plans, process documents, daily briefs, weekly updates, decision memos, launch kits, financial reviews, and workflow audits.
- The handoff should include source materials, constraints, review expectations, and destination format.
- The team then checks evidence, refines judgment, resolves open questions, and decides what happens next.
