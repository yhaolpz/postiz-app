## Editorial decisions

- Preserve the source attribution in the spoken script where it establishes the method's authority.
- Build one reusable Decision-Ready Research Brief with six fields: decision and audience; question and scope; research plan and source strategy; evidence standard; gaps and confidence; deliverable and next action.
- Do not claim that Deep Research guarantees correctness or that every research task requires the deeper mode.
- Omit product-navigation details and duplicated prompt examples that do not add a new method, boundary, or evidence rule.
