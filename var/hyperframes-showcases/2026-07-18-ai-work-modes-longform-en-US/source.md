## Confirmed source facts

- The 2026 Work Trend Index surveyed 20,000 AI-using workers in ten countries and analyzed Microsoft 365 work signals.
- Human intensity and agent intensity are independent dimensions, not opposite ends of one scale.
- The source names four work modes: asking, collaboration, exploration, and delegation.
- 49% of Copilot conversations supported cognitive work; the remaining categories were working with people (19%), finding information (15%), and producing work (17%).
- 66% of respondents said AI gave them more time for high-value work, and 58% said it helped them produce work they could not have produced one year earlier.
- 86% treated AI output as a starting point and still saw themselves as responsible for the final result.
- Quality control and critical thinking were among the human capabilities respondents expected to matter more.
