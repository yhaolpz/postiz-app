| 40.94–49.80s | The viewer leaves with four fields to keep visible | Verification pose | One decision card | Verdict is highlighted near the end |

The English timing is compiled from the final Ana `+30%` TTS and VTT. Audible utterance boundaries drive the edit: the question-to-promise pause is `0.45s`; every other inter-utterance pause is `0.15–0.25s`. This v2 sample includes the requested up-front follow-and-save cue but no closing CTA, chapter rail, publishing action, or production-baseline update.
